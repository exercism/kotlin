# Hints

## General

## 1. Reply to a correct guess

- You can match a specific value in a [when][kotlin-when] expression or statement.

## 2. Reply to a close guess

- You can match [multiple values][kotlin-when-multiple-values] on a branch.

## 3. Reply to too low guesses

- The [when][kotlin-when] expression or statement has a way to match anything else that is not matched by the branches.

## 4. Reply to too high guesses

- The [when][kotlin-when] expression or statement has a way to match anything else that is not matched by the branches.
- You may need to adjust one of the branches from a previous task.

## 5. Reply to a close guess after more than 5 guesses

- There is a [way][kotlin-when-guard-conditions] to add conditions to branches.
- Remember, the order of the branches is important!
